## What are Exe 6 Apps?

Apps are extensions and add-ons for Exe 6 that expand your experience and add more features and functionalities. The Exe 6 ecosystem allows developers to create powerful extensions that integrate seamlessly with your admin panel.

## Getting Started with Apps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the App Library">
    Navigate to **Exe 6 Onboarding** to browse available apps
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Your App">
    Explore the library to find apps that match your needs
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install">
    Click the install button on any app to add it to your Exe 6 setup
  </Step>
</Steps>
